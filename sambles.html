Our Services

TMDS Provides Services

We provide comprehensive underwater and marine solutions across multiple industries. Our expert team delivers professional services with cutting-edge technology and unparalleled expertise in UAE's onshore and offshore environments.

Marine Services

We provide expert underwater inspections, including non-destructive testing (NDT), diving inspections, and videography to evaluate the condition of marine structures. Using advanced equipment, we perform both routine checks and targeted inspections. Our services cover pipelines, offshore rigs, and underwater constructions.

Construction

We specialize in building and maintaining durable marine infrastructure like quay walls, seawalls, bridge piers, and underwater structures. Our expert team employs advanced techniques to complete projects safely and efficiently, whether for new development, ongoing maintenance, or emergency repairs, for both onshore and offshore needs.
